Best Movies According To Me

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

The Conjuring

This was the first horror movie that really scared me. Plus I love a good demoic posession and exorcism.

Eastern Promise

I watched this during my final year of university living in London, and the mafia setting and storyline seemed so real.

Interstellar

This movie made me cry! The relationships were perfectly crafted and real, and the science made sense even when it was too technical for me to even comprehend.
